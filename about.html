The Founder

Lukas Kalasho

Founder, Tonight's Reserved — San Diego, CA

Lukas Kalasho was born and raised in San Diego, and has spent his entire life in the restaurant business — growing up around kitchens, dining rooms, and the day-to-day grind of running a restaurant, long before he ever built a marketing agency.

That hands-on experience runs the operations behind restaurants like Banbu Sushi, where Lukas took over the online marketing and grew its social following to over 60,000 followers — turning an underutilized Instagram and Facebook presence into a real driver of reservations and walk-ins.

Tonight's Reserved was built on a simple realization: most restaurants aren't struggling because of bad food or bad service — they're struggling because nobody's fighting for their visibility online. Lukas started this agency to be the partner he wished existed when he was running restaurants himself — one that actually understands the floor, the kitchen, and what it takes to turn an empty Tuesday into a fully booked night.
